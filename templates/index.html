Chest Pain First Aid

Follow these steps immediately while waiting for help

1
Stay Calm & Sit Down

Stop all activity. Sit in a comfortable position. Do not lie flat.

2
Call Emergency Services

Call 108 immediately. Tell them you have chest pain.

3
Take Aspirin (if prescribed)

Chew one 325mg aspirin if your doctor has prescribed it.

4
Loosen Tight Clothing

Remove tie, unbutton shirt collar, loosen belt.

5
Slow Deep Breaths

Breathe in slowly for 4 counts, hold 2, exhale for 6.

6
Unlock Door for Paramedics

If alone, unlock your door so help can reach you.

How to measure BP at home

Home BP monitors are widely available and easy to use. Follow these simple steps:

  • Wear the cuff on your upper arm while seated and relaxed.
  • Rest for 5 minutes before taking a reading.
  • Keep your arm at heart level and avoid talking during measurement.
  • Take 2-3 readings and use the average value.

These devices are affordable and often available at pharmacies, online stores, or medical shops.

How to get your cholesterol tested

Cholesterol requires a simple lab test called a lipid profile.

  • Visit a nearby diagnostic lab or hospital.
  • Ask for a lipid profile / cholesterol test.
  • Typical cost in India is around ₹200–₹500.
  • Some pharmacies and clinics also offer finger-prick cholesterol checks.

If you do not know your cholesterol, the app uses an average value so the prediction remains usable.
